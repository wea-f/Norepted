Youtube Watcher

How To Use:

Paste your youtube video link in the orange box and press the LAUNCH button (or press the Enter key).

Features:

Press + or - to increase/decrease video size. Press ↺ to reset your changes.

Press 0 to exit faster and hide history.

For a Tab Disguise (into Google Drive), press 1.

To save a video, press the "COPY" button and bookmark the copied link.





The video(s) will show down below
