v1.7

Welcome to Norepted

v1.7 is a massive update featuring a brand-new multi-tab browser, Youtube Shorts support, and a totally revamped UI.

  • 🌐
    Multi-Tab Browser Access the web directly inside with the new proxy-based browser.
  • 📱
    Shorts Scroller Dive into YouTube Shorts with full scroll integration and backup viewing sources.
  • 🔍
    In-App YouTube Search Securely search for videos directly on the Watch and Shorts page.
  • 🔄
    Smart Source Switching
    Some filters block certain sources. If a video or proxy fails, press the shuffle (🔄) button to instantly try another one.
  • ⚡
    Smarter & Faster Accidentally closed a video? Hit the "Undo" pop-up. Slow? Performance mode automatically turns on.
